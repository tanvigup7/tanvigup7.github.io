The hands are still, the ticking heart lies flat, No chime remains to cradle the rise and fall of day. Its gentle tweet once rose with the sun, Now silence folds, a blanket enveloping what’s lost. Away, away, away. It marked our days, with threads of quiet time, Endless hours at sea, Away, away, away. Each second ticks, the stitch pulls, the sail falls further. Away, away, away. We never watched it closely, So sure it would go on. Unmeasured hours, drift untouched. It feels oddly wide. The wall is bare. We mourn the ticking we ignored, the seconds we let slip, Away, away, away.
